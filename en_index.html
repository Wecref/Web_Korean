Chaipang Korean

Learn the principles of Hangul through fun games and activities,
and build the confidence to read and write on your own.

  • Start with consonants and vowels to understand the foundations of Hangul.
  • Combine consonants and vowels to form syllables.
  • Learn the sounds and usage of final consonants.
  • Understand how syllables are formed and learn to read Korean words.
  • Build vocabulary through words and illustrations.
  • Complete your learning journey by practicing writing Hangul.
